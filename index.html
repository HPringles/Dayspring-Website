ABOUT US

We have been playing for over 30 years now and there are currently three sections to the band :

Dayspring 1 and 2

This is the main part of the band. It is made up of children from the age of 7 to around 20 years old. Dayspring 1 is the group of generally older, more experiences players. This is the band you will mainly see at performances.

Dayspring 2 is made up of either the younger, less experienced players, or some that are not available enough to play in Dayspring 1. This band is seen at many concerts , however at smaller events they are not often around.

Both bands meet on a tuesday at Earlsdon Methodist Church coventry, and are always looking for new players to join dayspring 2. If this interests you, you can use the contact form below to ask any questions you may have

Friday Band

This band is run on a friday morning, and is for adults. This band has been setup recently and in run by Steve Fox, who ran the main Dayspring 1 and 2 band for many years beforehand and has lots of experience in teaching the instrument.

ReSprung

Resprung is another recent addition to our set of bands. It is made up of ex-band members from the earlier bands. Resprung are mainly seen at big events such as anniversary concerts or large events.
